Overall Design Process

For tackling and solving problems that occurs specifically to language learners I carried out the 5 stages of the Design Thinking model in a perpetual loop:

  • Empathise
  • Define
  • Ideate
  • Prototype
  • Iterate

Empathise

To gain profound insights from language learners I interviewed six people who learned a foreign language in the last six months. My research goal was to understand what motivates intermediate language learners and why they are frustrated with foreign language learning.

Define

Analysing and synthesizing the gathered research data allowed me to narrow down to the prior design question of: How might we help language learners to feel more confident to practice and improve their language skills?

Ideate

To generate design ideas I created mind maps and dozen of sketches of solution for my user personas and their specific flow.

Prototype

Next I transferred selected ideas from the previous stage to different level of fidelity.

Iterate

To ensure that my design solution is feasible and viable I carried out several feedback and testing rounds. The gathered results lead to refinements of the current design but also to redefine former problems.
